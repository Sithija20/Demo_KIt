Assembly and Disassembly Instructions

Please read these instructions carefully before assembling or disassembling the kit. Use the diagrams to identify the necessary parts.

Assembling Gears and Pinions

Take the base and place it on a stable surface. Identify the holes where the gears will be mounted.

Take the first gear with Involute teeth, mount it on the corresponding shaft, and attach it to the base. Ensure it rotates freely.

Note: Ensure correct Clearance and Center Distance are maintained when mounting gears.

Disassembly

To disassemble the kit, follow the assembly steps in reverse order. Carefully remove all parts and store them safely.
